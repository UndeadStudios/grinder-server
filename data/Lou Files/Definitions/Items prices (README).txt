items.json - Holds all the real items data such as examine, name, data, and the prices they have is used for shops.
item_prices.json - Has original osrs g.e prices for the items and is used for items on death, repairing costs, and carriedwealth in wild.
price_checker.json - We have implemented all player given prices for our traded items such as the market price and we use it for in-game real price checker, looting bag total items worth so
			use it just to know the game items prices.
osrs_store.json - Prices for the OSRS store includes buying and selling items to the price. Note: Selling price is 1/4th the original price.
